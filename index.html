About Us

Integrity. Transparency. Competence

Poonawalla Finance Private Limited is a financial services company, registered with the Reserve Bank of India as a Non-Deposit Accepting Systemically Important Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC). Headquartered in Pune, we are a part of the $10 billion Cyrus Poonawalla Group.

The name stands for Trust, Passion for Business and Excellence; which is now creating new benchmarks as a NBFC. We are a digital-lending NBFC and offer a suite of loans to enable our customers to accomplish their financial requirements.

Our products comprise of small ticket size Personal Loan, Professional Loan and Unsecured Business Loan, which are tailored to meet the customer's requirement; in a bid to offer maximum value. We aim to create a social impact and help in serving the financial needs of our customers.
